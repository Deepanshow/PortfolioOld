  • Cancer cell detection using transfer learning I predicted a human cell to either "Benign" or "Malignant" using a dataset of 10000 images.

    Transfer Learning from the EfficientNet and InceptionNet was used.
    EfficientNet was found to be efficient and required less epoch time compared to InceptionNet.
    Adamax was compared with NAdam optimizer where NAdam showed faster convergence rate.
    Further custom learning rate was used for convergence of the EfficientNet model with Nadam Optimizer, resulting in 95% classification accuracy.
